Web Development

When I grow up, I want to be a Web Developer. This is because I enjoy computer science and web development. A web developer designs and develops webpages and websites for companies or people. They oftentimes make up most of the layout of the webpage, before other developers help them.

Salary

A web developer can make between $67,000 to $110,000. It depends on the experience of the web developer and the company that they work for.

Education

A web developer needs at least a bachelors degree from university, however, computer science experience is also good.

Skills

A web developer needs experience developing webpages, needs to be hard working, and needs to be organized. A good web developer is also ready to dedicate their time to the company or person they work for, since web development can be a time-intensive job.

Other Jobs

I could also work in other jobs in the computer science industry such as programming or IT.

Northwestern has good amenities. It has gym equipment, open gym hours, and large dorms.

Loyola has great amenities too. It has good dorms and study lounges, as well as a fitness center.
